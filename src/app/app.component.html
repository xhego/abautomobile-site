Terms and conditions

Vehicle repairs, call-outs and workshop terms.

These terms apply to diagnostics, servicing, repairs, mobile call-outs and workshop work carried out by AB's Auto Mobile Mechanic (Pty) Ltd.

Quotes and authorisation

Prices given before work begins are estimates unless confirmed as a final written quote. No additional work, parts or labour will be carried out without customer approval by quote, WhatsApp, phone, email, written instruction or verbal instruction.

Diagnostics and inspection

A diagnostic, inspection or call-out fee may apply and will be communicated before the work is carried out. Some faults may only become visible after further testing, dismantling or repair work.

Payment and release

Payment is due when the agreed work is completed and before the vehicle, keys or parts are released, unless otherwise agreed in writing. Deposits may be required for special-order parts.

Collection and storage

Customers must collect the vehicle within the agreed period after being notified that work is complete. Storage is charged at R250 per day if a vehicle is not collected within 2 days after completion notice, unless another collection arrangement is agreed in writing.

Parts and replaced items

Where practical, replaced parts may be returned to the customer on request, unless the customer waives this, the part is subject to a warranty or core exchange claim, or safe/legal disposal is required.

Warranty and exclusions

Workmanship and supplied parts are handled in line with applicable South African consumer protection laws. Warranty does not cover misuse, neglect, overheating, incorrect fluids, racing, unauthorised modifications, customer-supplied defective parts, or third-party work after repair.

Mobile call-outs

Mobile work depends on availability, travel distance, weather, safety and access to a suitable working area. If a repair cannot be completed safely on-site, the vehicle may need to move to the workshop or another suitable repair location.

Disputes and acceptance

AB's Auto Mobile Mechanic (Pty) Ltd aims to resolve concerns fairly and directly. If a matter cannot be resolved, customers may approach the Motor Industry Ombudsman of South Africa where applicable. Booking a service or authorising work means these terms are accepted.
